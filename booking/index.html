The Players

Real Musicians.
Real Credentials.

A core of seasoned players with deep roots in rock, reggae, blues, and beyond — united by a shared obsession with great music and the moments that only happen live. 2–8 piece band depending on your event and budget.

Dean Palermo
Guitar / Vocals · Founder

Primary guitarist and founder. Blistering leads, laid-back funk, Jeff Beck-style invention, and a classic reggae chop developed over 15+ years working directly with Toots and the Maytals. Also produces with Stephen Marley's drummer Squidly Cole. By day, Dean runs Elettro.com — a top South Florida digital agency — meaning every Stashbox booking comes with professional-grade marketing built in.

Josh Clyde
Lead Vocals / Guitar

Youthful, electric, and impossible to ignore. His signature skill is in-the-moment improvised rap and banter woven around audience interaction — keeping people mystified, engaged, and dancing. Versatile enough to sing, rap, or flow across any genre. By day, the "Sandman" — South Florida's top international corporate sand sculptor.

Michael Hugh
Keyboards / Vocals

The sonic architect behind the keys. Runs the studio sessions as primary engineer — recording, mixing, and mastering. Tremendous vocal range and the technical depth of a seasoned producer. He's the reason the band sounds as good on tape as they do on stage.

Peter Roland
Guitar

An absolutely killer guitarist whose playing elevates every room he's in. Deep blues sensibility and serious chops — the kind of player whose solos make people stop mid-conversation and just listen. Also performing with Kat Riggins Blues Revival USA.

Gary "G Money" Williams
Bass

One of the most jaw-dropping funk bass players you will ever witness live. His bass solos are the kind of once-in-a-room experience that gets audiences wild with excitement — phones out, filming and sharing. A world-class player who organically creates viral moments every time.

David Nizri
Drums

The engine of the operation — a drummer with the feel to serve the song and the chops to elevate it. David's groove is the foundation everything else is built on, holding the band in the pocket through genre shifts and improvisational moments.

Mike Ancona
Bass

Low-end locked and deeply musical. Whether it's a reggae one-drop, a funk pocket, or a blues groove — he's the glue that holds the whole thing together.

Jarvis Jones
Drums

A pro's pro behind the kit — always gigging, zero stylistic boundaries. Rooted in the church drumming tradition, he carries that deep soul and pocket in everything he plays. His command of funk, soul, and high-energy rhythm keeps the crowd locked in.

Bob Cleary
Bass

A legendary South Florida bassist with decades of stage miles. Most frequently performing with the Bobby Nathan Band. He brings the kind of deep, road-earned groove that only comes from a lifetime of gigging.

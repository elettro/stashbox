What It Is
01

Custom Music Player

A branded player for audio, video, song metadata, likes, shares, play counts, and direct fan interaction.

02

Visual Song Experience

Each song can pull from its own image and video clip folders, turning playback into a moving media experience.

03

Full CMS Control

A backend system manages songs, artwork, videos, visibility, product placements, ad spots, and performance stats.

Why send every fan back to someone else’s platform?

Large platforms create reach. Owned media creates control, data, commerce, and direct audience value.

Built for direct monetization.

Use media attention to promote products, sponsors, events, merch, campaigns, artist drops, and partner offers.

Core Platform Capabilities
Song-level image and video clip folders for richer playback experiences.
Shopify product spots directly under the media player.
Internal ad system for brand messages, sponsors, and campaigns.
Backend tracking for plays, likes, shares, ad activity, and product engagement.
Artist, genre, mood, album, and release metadata built into the experience.
Designed as an owned destination, not another generic embedded playlist.

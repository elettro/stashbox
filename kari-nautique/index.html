ABOUT KARI NAUTIQUE

Kari Nautique is a Florida-native turned global fitness and bikini model, author, painter, and fully-sober influencer. Based in Miami, she brings elite modeling experience and a high-profile lifestyle to every project — flying worldwide to join top influencers, luxury brand campaigns, and major promotional events.

After years of behind-the-scenes singing, she has now stepped into the studio in a full music partnership with a longtime producer collaborator to launch her original music. Kari embodies creativity, strength, and transformation — on the runway, in the gym, in the studio, and in life.

ABOUT JOSH CLYDE

Josh Clyde is a Florida-based singer-songwriter, guitarist, and the dynamic lead vocalist of the South Florida reggae-rock band Stashbox. A self-taught musician since childhood, Clyde infuses his broad musical influences into the group's eclectic sound and songwriting. As co-frontman of Stashbox — led alongside guitarist Dean Palermo — he helps shape a creative fusion of genres, from laid-back reggae grooves to high-energy rock, blues, funk, and more.

Clyde's role spans lead vocals and rhythm guitar, and he is a driving force in the band's original songwriting and arrangements, contributing soulful melodies and catchy hooks to their tracks now released on major streaming platforms.

On stage, Josh Clyde is known for his high-energy, crowd-engaging performance style and signature vocal versatility. He delivers soulful singing with the ability to slip into spontaneous rap verses and improvised banter — bringing an electrifying, interactive element to Stashbox's live shows.

His frontman prowess — from pumping up dance-floor audiences to flowing off-the-cuff lyrics — has become a hallmark of the band's concerts. Clyde's charismatic presence and improvisational flair perfectly complement Palermo's guitar work, making for a powerful collaborative dynamic on stage. Whether rocking festival crowds or entertaining at resort venues, Josh Clyde's soulful intensity and genuine passion for music shine through, helping turn every Stashbox performance into an uplifting reggae-rock experience that leaves audiences dancing and craving more.

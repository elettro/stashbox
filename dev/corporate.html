Stashbox brings world-class live music to corporate events, product launches, brand activations, award nights, and private parties. We tailor every set to your event — from sophisticated background dinner music to full high-energy dance sets that have the whole room moving.

WHY STASHBOX

10+ years of professional live performance at resorts, festivals, casinos, and private venues across South Florida and beyond.

200+ songs across every genre and era — rock, reggae, blues, funk, jazz, soul, and originals. Something for every audience and every moment.

Full sound and production coordination available. We work with your venue and AV team to ensure a flawless show every time.

Customizable setlists for your audience and brand. Tell us the vibe, the crowd, and the energy you want — we'll build the set around it.

Tribute show options for themed events — Stashbox Does Dylan, Stashbox Does Sublime, Classic Reggae Show, and more.

Available in 2–8 piece configurations to fit your venue and budget — from intimate acoustic duo to full-band stage production.

Stashbox Does Sublime

Sublime Tribute Band for South Florida Events

Book Stashbox Does Sublime for South Florida festivals, beach events, resorts, private parties, breweries, and rock-reggae live music programs.

Book this show

Stashbox Does Sublime is a rock-reggae tribute set for audiences that want beach energy, singalong hooks, punk attitude, dub grooves, and a band that understands how to keep a room moving. The show is designed for South Florida venues, festivals, resorts, breweries, private parties, and waterfront events where Sublime songs naturally connect with reggae, ska, rock, and party crowds.

Stashbox is not a costume act. The band treats Sublime material as part of a larger live vocabulary that includes reggae, rock, blues, funk, soul, originals, classic reggae, and jam-ready arrangements. That makes the set flexible: it can be a dedicated Sublime tribute, a rock-reggae night, or a mixed event show with Sublime highlights placed exactly where the energy needs to lift.

For a Fort Lauderdale live band, Miami live band, Boca Raton live band, Palm Beach live band, Davie live band, or Florida Keys resort band request, Stashbox can scale the lineup to the room. Smaller configurations work for patios and private parties, while larger configurations can fill festival stages, casino entertainment calendars, beach resorts, and corporate event band South Florida programs.

The set can include beloved Sublime songs, reggae classics, roots grooves, and Stashbox originals. It pairs well with Stashbox Radio listening, video clips, and the public setlist library so bookers can shape the exact tone before the date.

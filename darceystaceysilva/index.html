ABOUT DARCEY & STACEY

Darcey and Stacey Silva are not only well-known television personalities but also entrepreneurs with a flair for music production. Best known for their appearances on TLC's 90 Day Fiancé and Darcey & Stacey, the twin sisters have expanded their creative horizons into music, collaborating with notable producers to bring their musical vision to life.

Currently, Darcey and Stacey are working closely with Stashbox Studios — co-producing and collaborating on tracks designed for mass distribution. Their collaboration with Stashbox blends their pop sensibilities with Stashbox's diverse musical influences, resulting in tracks that are catchy, empowering, and primed for a wide audience.

Together, they are crafting songs that are set to resonate across various platforms, further solidifying their presence in the entertainment industry beyond their television and fashion careers.

Stashbox is a South Florida live band built for the stage — high energy, genre-spanning, and always locked into the room. From intimate bar sets to festival main stages, we bring the same commitment to every performance.

The band plays original music alongside deep-catalog covers spanning rock, reggae, blues, funk, and Americana. Tribute productions — Stashbox Does Dylan, Stashbox Does Sublime, and our Classic Reggae show — are full-scale, fully rehearsed performances with period-appropriate arrangements and production.

We come fully self-contained with a professional PA system and can scale from 2 to 8 pieces depending on the venue and budget. Studio recordings were tracked at Studio One, Fort Lauderdale, FL.

What We Do

Tribute Production

Stashbox Does Dylan

A full-band deep dive into Bob Dylan's catalog — folk-rock classics, mid-period anthems, and late-era masterworks. 19 tracks recorded live at Studio One and released as an LP in February 2026. Spanning five decades: folk, rock, blues, country, and Americana. From Blowin' in the Wind to Things Have Changed.

Before You Book

FAQ

What types of events do you play? +

Stashbox plays festivals, casinos, resorts, private parties, corporate events, city concerts, and sporting events. We've played outdoor stages, hotel ballrooms, beach venues, and everything in between. If you have a stage and an audience, we're in.

How many musicians come with the band? +

Stashbox scales from 2 to 8 pieces depending on your venue size, stage, and budget. A duo or trio is great for intimate venues and cocktail hours. The full 8-piece — guitar, bass, drums, keys, percussion, sax, second guitar, and vocals — is built for festival main stages and large event spaces.

We'll work with you to find the right configuration for your event.

Do you bring your own PA system? +

Yes. Stashbox is fully self-contained with a professional PA system. We don't need you to supply a sound system or a sound engineer for most events. For very large outdoor stages, we can integrate with your existing production — just let us know what you have and we'll coordinate.

Can we customize the setlist? +

Absolutely. We have 200+ songs in the catalog across rock, reggae, blues, funk, and originals. You can request specific songs, pick a genre focus, or tell us the vibe you want and let us build the set. The tribute shows (Dylan, Sublime, Classic Reggae) have dedicated setlists, but the mix show is fully customizable.

What is the Stashbox Does Dylan show? +

It's a full-scale tribute to Bob Dylan spanning five decades of his catalog — from early folk and protest songs through his rock period, the country albums, and his late-career masterpieces. We recorded a 19-track live LP at Studio One in Fort Lauderdale in 2026. The show is fully arranged, fully rehearsed, and performs the material with real reverence for Dylan's songwriting.

If you want a sophisticated, emotionally resonant tribute production, this is the one.

How far in advance do we need to book? +

For weekends and peak season dates (winter and spring in South Florida), 60–90 days ahead is ideal. We do take last-minute bookings when the calendar allows, so it's always worth reaching out even if your event is soon. Contact us and we'll let you know availability immediately.

Do you travel outside of South Florida? +

Yes. Stashbox is based in South Florida but available for events anywhere. We've played events across Florida and travel nationally for festivals, casino engagements, and destination events. Travel and accommodation details are factored into the booking quote.

How do I get a quote or check availability? +

The fastest way is to call or text 310.408.6687. You can also email booking@stashbox.com with your event date, venue, and a note about what you're looking for. We respond quickly and can usually get you a quote the same day.

Elettro does not guarantee minimum plays, placement, promotion, audience size, revenue, advertising, reporting frequency, or continued availability.

The Agreement begins on the Effective Date in the signature section and continues until terminated. Either party may terminate future use by written notice. Termination does not affect rights and obligations arising before removal, including warranties, indemnity, payment duties, dispute provisions, or the continued archival retention of agreements and rights records.

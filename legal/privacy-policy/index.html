2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • name, email address, phone number, company, artist name, and other contact details
  • messages, booking inquiries, support requests, rights claims, and legal notices
  • artist submissions, music and video metadata, ownership information, credits, release details, and signed agreements
  • account, playlist, favorite, or preference information if account features are introduced

Information collected automatically

  • IP address, browser, device type, operating system, language, approximate location, referring page, and timestamps
  • pages viewed, searches, filters, songs selected, playback starts, listening duration, completed plays, likes, shares, share visits, video interactions, and product clicks
  • cookie, local-storage, session, security, diagnostic, and error information

Information from third parties

We may receive limited information from hosting, analytics, embedded-media, ecommerce, social, email, security, and content-delivery providers, as well as from artists, managers, labels, publishers, and authorized representatives.

3. How We Use Information

  • operate, secure, maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the website, radio player, video experiences, and content systems
  • deliver requested music, video, product, search, filter, sharing, and playback functions
  • measure audience activity, catalog performance, product interest, and technical reliability
  • respond to bookings, submissions, support requests, privacy requests, and rights complaints
  • verify content ownership, permissions, licenses, releases, and contributor authority
  • detect fraud, artificial traffic, abuse, infringement, security incidents, and violations of our terms
  • comply with law, enforce agreements, establish legal claims, and protect users, artists, Stashbox, and third parties

4. Legal Bases Where Required

Where a law requires a legal basis, we process information based on performance of a contract, legitimate interests in operating and protecting the services, compliance with legal obligations, consent, or another basis recognized by applicable law. You may withdraw consent for future processing where consent is the applicable basis.

5. How We Disclose Information

We may disclose information to:

  • cloud hosting, database, storage, content-delivery, analytics, email, security, development, and support providers working for us
  • embedded-media, social, ecommerce, advertising, and merchandise providers when you use their features or follow their links
  • artists, managers, labels, publishers, rights administrators, and representatives when needed to manage authorized content or resolve a rights issue
  • government authorities, courts, law enforcement, regulators, and other parties when legally required or reasonably necessary to protect rights and safety
  • a buyer, investor, lender, insurer, adviser, or successor in a merger, financing, acquisition, restructuring, or sale involving the business

We do not sell personal information for money. We do not knowingly use sensitive personal information to infer characteristics about visitors.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies for essential operation, preferences, security, playback, analytics, and related functions. Embedded services may use their own technologies. Review and update available choices on the Cookie Policy and Preferences page.

7. Retention

We retain information for the period reasonably needed for the purpose collected, including service operation, security, analytics, rights documentation, contract administration, dispute resolution, tax or accounting duties, and legal compliance. Retention periods vary by record type. Signed licenses, ownership certifications, takedown records, and related evidence may be retained longer to preserve a chain of rights and legal history.

8. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No internet transmission, storage system, or security measure guarantees absolute security.

9. Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live and whether a law applies to Elettro Incorporated, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, objection, or information about disclosures. You may also have a right to withdraw consent and to appeal a denied request.

Submit a request to dpalermo@elettro.com. Include “Stashbox Privacy Request” in the subject and describe the request. We may verify identity and authority before completing it.

10. California Privacy Notice

California residents may have rights to know, delete, correct, opt out of sale or sharing, limit certain uses of sensitive personal information, and receive equal service after exercising privacy rights. These rights apply only when the relevant California law covers the business and the information.

Stashbox does not sell personal information for money. If Stashbox later engages in activity defined as “sharing” for cross-context behavioral advertising under applicable California law, it will provide the required opt-out method and honor legally recognized browser-based opt-out preference signals, including Global Privacy Control, where required.

12. International Processing

Information may be processed in the United States and other countries where service providers operate. Those locations may have different privacy laws. Where required, we use recognized transfer mechanisms or other lawful safeguards.

The Technical Foundation

Four Pillars.
One Owned Platform.

Stashbox Radio is not a streaming service. It's a private-label music and commerce ecosystem built on AWS infrastructure — giving brands full control over sound, visuals, ads, and data.

01 — Content

Full-Scale Song CMS

Every track has a dedicated profile: metadata, genre, mood, visibility, audio URL, artwork, and linked video. Centralized management with granular filtering by artist, album, mood, and genre.

  • Reggae, Rock, Soul, Funk, Blues, Calypso, Electronic
  • Browser-based RDS Integration (AWS)
  • Granular mood tagging (Chill, Energetic, Spiritual)

02 — Visuals

Visual Experience Controller

Beyond static artwork. The VEC pulls from curated Visual Library Folders to create a dynamic moving slideshow while each song plays — lifestyle clips, performance footage, or brand visuals.

  • Approved media folders per track or brand
  • Music-video quality without full production cost
  • Randomized "visual recipes" per brand category

03 — Commerce

Integrated Commerce & Ad Engine

Every song page is a commercial storefront. Song-specific Shopify product links appear directly in the player. Automated merch fallbacks ensure no revenue opportunity is ever missed.

  • Direct Shopify product placement per track
  • YouTube-style ad inventory (30–90 second spots)
  • Customizable skip-logic and ad frequency controls

04 — Analytics

Owned Data & Performance

Close the attribution loop. Track plays, likes, shares, skips, and ad engagement. See exactly which songs drive product clicks and purchases. Your data, never shared with streaming giants.

  • 10+ key performance metrics per song
  • Ad click-through, skip rate, "Learn More" engagement
  • Direct commerce attribution from stream to purchase

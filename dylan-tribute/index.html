Stashbox Does Dylan

Bob Dylan Tribute Band for South Florida Events

Book Stashbox Does Dylan, a South Florida Bob Dylan tribute show for festivals, theaters, resorts, casinos, private events, and listening-room concerts.

Book this show

Stashbox Does Dylan is a dedicated tribute show built for listeners who want the songs treated as living performances rather than museum pieces. Stashbox brings a full-band South Florida approach to Bob Dylan material, balancing respect for the writing with the energy of a working rock, reggae, blues, and funk band.

The show works for theaters, listening rooms, casinos, resorts, festivals, breweries, cultural events, private parties, and corporate gatherings that want recognizable songs with real musicianship. It can be delivered as a focused Bob Dylan tribute concert or folded into a broader Stashbox mix show for audiences that also want reggae, blues, funk, originals, and classic rock.

Stashbox recorded a 19-track Dylan tribute collection at Studio One in Fort Lauderdale, with related pages for Stashbox Does Dylan, The Man In Me, Visions of Johanna, and Blood in My Eyes. That deeper catalog gives bookers a clear view of the band's commitment to the repertoire.

For South Florida events, the Dylan show can fit Fort Lauderdale, Davie, Miami, Boca Raton, Palm Beach, Florida Keys resort dates, casino and resort live music programs, and destination private events. The set can lean acoustic and lyrical, electric and bluesy, or festival-ready depending on room size and audience.

Bookers can request a duo, trio, quartet, or larger band configuration. Stashbox can also combine Dylan highlights with Stashbox originals, classic reggae, Sublime material, and rock staples for a custom event arc. Use the booking page to check dates and discuss production needs.
